Who We Are

FASHION AS A STATEMENT

SAP Design Store was born in the heart of Cape Town with a single conviction: your clothes should say something. Not whisper — say something bold, confident, and unapologetically you.

We design for people who walk into a room and need no introduction. Every piece in our collection is built with intention, crafted with care, and cut for the kind of confidence that can't be faked.

From our Cape Town studio to your wardrobe — we handle every stitch, seam, and silhouette with pride. We manufacture locally, support South African makers, and reinvest in the communities that inspire our work.

How to Measure

Use a soft measuring tape and measure directly over your undergarments. Keep the tape snug but not tight. Chest: measure around the fullest part. Waist: measure at your natural waistline. Hips: measure around the fullest part of your hips.

Fit Notes

Our dresses and tops are cut with a relaxed fit — they run slightly larger. Trousers and bottoms are true to size. Outerwear is designed to layer — we suggest sizing up if you plan to wear thick knitwear underneath.

Delivery & Returns

SHIPPING & RETURNS

STANDARD DELIVERY

Orders are dispatched within 1–2 business days. Standard delivery across South Africa takes 3–5 business days via our courier partners. Free shipping on all orders over R800.

EXPRESS DELIVERY

Need it fast? Express next-day delivery is available for Cape Town, Johannesburg, and Durban. Order before 12pm for same-day dispatch. Additional fee of R120 applies.

30-DAY RETURNS

Not happy? Return any unworn, unwashed item in its original packaging within 30 days for a full refund or exchange. Simply contact us and we'll arrange a collection.
